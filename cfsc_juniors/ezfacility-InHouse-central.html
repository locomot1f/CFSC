2020 Fall Session II
AGES 6 -- 8 Boys & Girls

LOCATION Force - Richmond
PRICE $105 for 6 weeks
This program is a great introduction to small-sided soccer games with the assistance of guided discovery themes to help the learning process. The program structure ensures players are maximizing touches on the ball, learning how to solve soccer related problems and having lots of fun! Games will be led each week by Force Soccer Academy trainers who will provide instruction to the athletes. Players will sign-up as individuals and be grouped weekly to maximize the learning and playing experience by CFSA staff.  We highly recommend a size 3 or 4 soccer ball for this age group; a size 1 soccer ball for @ home. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
  • Athletic or Soccer Shoes (Cleats ARE Allowed)
  • Soccer Ball Size 3 or 4
