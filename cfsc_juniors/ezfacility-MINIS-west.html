This program is not only an excellent way to introduce your little athlete to soccer, but an awesome way to introduce the different dimensions of learning. Through this environment we look to activate learning through:
  • Mobility
  • Decision Making
  • Listening skills
... within the game of soccer. Experienced Cleveland Force Soccer Academy Trainers will work to build a solid foundation of a love for the ball!  We highly recommend a size 3 soccer ball for this age group; a size 1 soccer ball for @ home. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
