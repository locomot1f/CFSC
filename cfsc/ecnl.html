Cleveland Force SC & Internationals SC provide elite league competition through the Elite Clubs National League - Regional Division. 

We compete in the Ohio Valley Conference, facing off against teams from Ohio, Indiana, Kentucky, Tennessee, Western New York and Western Pennsylvania. 
Spring is the primary competition season .
