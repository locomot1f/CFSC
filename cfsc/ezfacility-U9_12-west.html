This program is built around the Dutch concept of play-making soccer. Some of the key ingredients of this style of soccer are: confidence, adaptability, concentration and knowing to attack as soon as the ball is won. This is an instructional clinic designed to promote these concepts through competition and task mastery training. Players will play small sided (5v5, 4v4, 3v3) games during each training session. This programmed is designed to be a supplemental training program to help improve each player's individual technical ability and soccer IQ. Experienced Cleveland Force Soccer Academy Trainers will work to build a solid foundation of agility, coordination, sportsmanship and listening skills. We highly recommend a size 4 soccer ball for this age group. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
  • Athletic or Soccer Shoes (Cleats ARE Allowed)
  • Soccer Ball Size 4
