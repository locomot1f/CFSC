🔻WEEKLY SUPPLEMENTAL CLINICS - DEVELOPING PLAYERS
This program is not only an excellent way to introduce your little athlete to soccer, but an awesome way to introduce the different dimensions of learning. Through this environment we look to activate learning through:
  • Mobility
  • Decision Making
  • Listening skills
... within the game of soccer. Experienced Cleveland Force Soccer Academy Trainers will work to build a solid foundation of a love for the ball! We highly recommend a size 3 soccer ball for this age group; and a size 1 soccer ball for @ home. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.

The Cleveland Force SC Juniors Academy is a school year program designed for young players that are beginning to show a great love for the game, and those families looking for a challenging environment, with more advanced coaching. The program is comprised of male and female athletes between 6 â€” 8 years of age.
The Program's primary objective is to maximize the development of every child in each of the four elements of the game in a FUN & SAFE environment!
  • Technical - ball manipulation
  • Physical/ Mobility - coordination, balance
  • Mental - decision making, spacial recognition
  • Tactical - teamwork
