This program is geared to introduce our players and families into our 4 pillars of player development:
  • Mental
  • Physical (Mobility)
  • Technical
  • Tactical
This programmed is designed to be a supplemental training program to help improve each player's individual technical ability and soccer IQ. Experienced Cleveland Force Soccer Academy Trainers will work to build a solid foundation of agility, coordination, sportsmanship and listening skills.  We highly recommend a size 4 or 5 soccer ball for this age group. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
  • Athletic or Soccer Shoes (Cleats ARE Allowed)
  • Soccer Ball Size 3 or 4
