Cleveland Force SC and Internationals SC Boys Academy Join Boys Elite Clubs National League
Cleveland, OH (April 29, 2021)

Our Boys Academy Program is excited to announce our acceptance into the Elite Clubs National League (ECNL) for the 2021-2022 season under the Cleveland Force SC banner.

This move will allow Cleveland Force SC to continue to provide the highest level of national competitive league programming, with two varied seasonal calendar options:

  • 10-month Super Cup for U15-U19 and conventional 10-month for U13-U14 ages
  • 7-month National Competition for U15-U19 (post HS participation)

In addition, our U10-U12 players will continue to participate in the Pre-ECNL regional league which provides Zone 1 players with advanced competition against other ECNL clubs.

“We are excited and humbled to be joining ECNL Boys for this upcoming season. Our mission is to create an unparalleled development environment for our players and this acceptance immensely supports our core mission. This opportunity allows our club to fully integrate our talented player pools while also further supporting our players and their local community programs. We look forward to working with the other clubs in the league to create the best environment possible for the soccer players of Northeast Ohio."
- Everett Palache, CFSC Director of Coaching

After many years as founding members of the former Boys Development Academy and current MLS Next Academy leagues, we have made the decision that the ECNL platform best serves our players, our club structure, and our community.

The Boys ECNL will be our top youth national competitive platform, of which will provide the following benets to our club’s players, parents, and staff:

  • National Competition platform with Regional Divisions and National Playoffs
  • National College Showcases for player exposure through 6 National Events
  • Player-Centered Identication Programs through the ECNL National Training Camp
  • Club and Coach Development Programs
  • Health and Education Resources
  • Players have the opportunity to play with their High School programs in the Fall AND participate in a top national league (7-month program)

Tryouts for the Boys ECNL National platform will begin May 3rd. CLICK HERE for more details on how to register.

Cleveland Force SC will be announcing more information in the near future, so please keep following us on our website and social media accounts.

The ECNL is the nation’s leading youth soccer development platform. The ECNL respects and celebrates the unique individuality of every youth soccer player and club, supporting and enabling them to unleash their unfettered passion and fierce tenacity in striving to achieve their potential.

The ECNL Mission: to Raise the Game is a total commitment to create the best possible environment for players, coaches, referees and administrators, with a determination to constantly question convention and challenge the status quo in youth sports.

The ECNL Commitment: We will lead, set our eyes on new horizons, and pursue them relentlessly. Born out of the belief in a better way. Continued in the ever-evolving pursuit of excellence.
