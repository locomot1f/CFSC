WHY SUPER Y LEAGUE FROM CFSC
We are excited to announce that our Cleveland Force SC Boys Program will be participating in Super Y League this Summer 2021! The decision to enter the Super Y League was done with our commitment to our Mission, Vision and Player Development Philosophy. Super Y League provides our members with an experience that is unparalleled in youth soccer. Super Y shares our passion for player development and realizes that in order to be an elite club, we must play in an elite league. With the new Regional Scouting Series, partnerships with some of the leaders in youth soccer, experienced and professional staff, advanced player tracking, and offering the chance to compete for a North American Championship, the Super Y League is the best operated youth league in North America, allowing it to stand above the competition.
YEARS OF PROVEN COMPETITION
The benefits that the league provides set the Super Y League and its member clubs apart. These benefits have been developed through 20 years as a mainstay in youth soccer.
  • Proven history of upholding the highest standards and accountability.
  • Year-round player involvement and development for clubs.
  • Access to unparalleled benefits through League Partners.
  • Increased exposure for member clubs and their players.
  • Opportunity to compete for a National Championship.
  • Greater collaboration between clubs, players and the League.
