Orange Soccer Club Program

Where Force Richmond (26555 Richmond Road, Bedford Hts.)
or Orange Village Park
Who Orange Community Residents
Age Boys & Girls born in 2012/2013
This CFSA program is specifically geared to prepare Orange Community players for the Fall 2021 outdoor league season. This progressive program combines both individual skill development and team concept mastery. There are 2 Options to choose from:
  1. March 14 & April 11- 4v4 friendly game play with Cleveland Force SC Junior Academy
    • Location: Force Richmond
    • Time: 10:00am-11:00am
  2. Spring 2021 Training & Games April/May
    1. 🔵 Weekly Outdoor Soccer Training w/ Force Staff
      • Location: Orange Village Park (Moves to Force Richmond if weather is inclement)
      • Tuesday Dates: April 20, 27, May 4, 11, 18, 25
      • Time: 6:30-7:30pm
    2. 🔵 5v5 League play against local communities
      • Location: Force Richmond
      • Saturdays: April 17,24 May 1,8,15, 22
      • Game Times: Between 11:00-1:00pm daily
Experienced Cleveland Force Soccer Academy Trainers will work to build a solid foundation of agility, coordination, sportsmanship and listening skills.  We highly recommend a size 4 soccer ball for this age group. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
  • Athletic or Soccer Shoes (Cleats ARE Allowed)
  • Soccer Ball Size 4
  • Water Bottle
