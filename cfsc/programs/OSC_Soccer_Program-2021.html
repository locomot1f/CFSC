Cleveland Force Soccer Academy
Orange Soccer Club
Spring 2021 Friendly Play Days
Where Force Richmond (26555 Richmond Road, Bedford Hts.)
Who Orange Community Residents
When March 14 & April 11
Age Boys & Girls born in 2012/2013
This CFSA program is specifically geared to prepare Orange Community players for the Fall 2021 outdoor league season. This progressive program combines both individual skill development and team concept mastery. There will be 2-25 minute games played of 4v4. We highly recommend a size 4 soccer ball for this age group. This program is also part of our Soccer Starts at Home (#SSAH) initiative.
EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
  • Athletic or Soccer Shoes (Cleats ARE Allowed)
  • Soccer Ball Size 4
  • Water Bottle
