Price Per Player
This program is based on subscription of packages within a Session. With our packages, the player has the opportunity to train at any of our geographical locations to get the full experience!
We now have new packages setup for more convenience to our customers. You are able to purchase individual packages (see Package break down below) to fit your schedule.
Families will be able to choose as many of the below 'Packages' at registration as they please.  So you can select; both west side, both east side, etc -- whatever your heart desires. The commitment is 8 weeks at $140 for the Fall Season per listed package. Once you sign up for a package, you are free to use them as you see fit. When you have used up the classes in your package, you will need to sign up for additional package(s). When you click to register you will see packages for the remaining year! The class packages will not be rolled over into the next Session.
There will also be the option for a Package including Games. Games will be used as an extension of the training to give real life experience for the players. Games will be played on Saturdays. Rosters will be set by the Coaching staff on a weekly basis.
