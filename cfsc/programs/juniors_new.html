What is the Juniors Academy Program?

The Cleveland Force SC Juniors Academy offers year-round programming designed for players looking for a challenging environment, with more advanced coaching. The program is comprised of male and female athletes between 5 — 8 years of age.

The Program's primary objective is to maximize the development of every child in each of the four elements of the game in a FUN & SAFE environment!

  • Technical - ball manipulation
  • Physical / Mobility - coordination, balance
  • Mental - decision making, spacial recognition
  • Tactical - teamwork

This is done by using proven methods of training to introduce new ideas and information for players. High repetition, high pace and pattern recognition activities separate the CFSC Juniors Academy from others in the area.

Furthermore, this program is an introduction to the Cleveland Force Soccer Club and opportunities to prepare players to advance into the full competitive program starting at U9. Players can attend training options in convenient geographical locations: East, West and South.

Through our progamming, we offer a multitude of Classrooms including:

  • Training / Classes
  • Camps / Specialty Clinics
  • Games within our JA Development League
