What is the Juniors Academy Program?

The Cleveland Force SC Juniors Academy is a year round program designed for young players that are beginning to show a great love for the game, and those families looking for a challenging environment, with more advanced coaching. The program is comprised of male and female athletes between 5 — 8 years of age.

The Program's primary objective is to maximize the development of every child in each of the four elements of the game in a FUN & SAFE environment!

  • Technical - ball manipulation
  • Physical/ Mobility - coordination, balance
  • Mental - decision making, spacial recognition
  • Tactical - teamwork

This is done by using progressive methods of training to introduce new ideas and information in a manner that is easy to understand and apply by our youngest players.

Furthermore, this program is an introduction to the Cleveland Force Soccer Club and opportunities to prepare players to advance into the full competitive program starting at U9. Players can attend training options in convenient geographical locations: East, West and South.

Through our progamming, we offer a multitude of Classrooms including:

  • Training / Classes
  • Camps / Specialty Clinics
  • Games within our JA Development League

Price Per Player

This program is based on subscription of packages within a Session. With our packages, the player has the opportunity to train at any of our geographical locations to get the full experience!
We now have new packages setup for more convenience to ourcustomers. You are able to purchase individual packages (see Package break down below) to fit your schedule.

Families will be able to choose as many of the below 'Packages' at registration as they please.  So you can select; both west side, both east side, etc -- whatever your heart desires. The commitment is 8 weeks at $140 for the Fall Season per listed package. Once you sign up for a package, you are free to use them as you see fit. When you have used up the classes in your package, you will need to sign up for additional package(s). When you click to register you will see packages for the remaining year!
The class packages will not be rolled over into the next Session.

There will also be the option for a Package including Games. Games will be used as an extension of the training to give real life experience for the players. Games will be played on Saturdays. Rosters will be set by the Coaching staff on a weekly basis.
