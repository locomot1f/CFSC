Where & When Is Training?

Athletes will be assigned to either Central or West locations for training up to 2-times per week based on their geographic home location.

Certain days & locations may have 2 Classrooms. Players will be assigned classrooms by the coaching staff to help maintain class structure, and maximize player development. You will only attend 1 class (unless otherwise specified by the coach).

If you have questions or concerns about specific classrooms, please do not hesitate to contact:
Coach Ben (ben@force-sports.com) or Coach Chris (chris@force-sports.com)
