The Cleveland Force Soccer Club & The Internationals Soccer Club have come together to combine its

next tier of male top players into the National Premier League under the Internationals charter member

status in the U.S. Club Soccer affiliated league.  The teams will play as Cleveland Internationals Soccer

Academy (CISA) and include 13 and 14 year old boys teams by birth year, respectively, in the fall season

and 13 through 18/19 year old boys teams by birth year, respectively,  in the spring season and share

qualified and experienced coaches from both programs.  The goal of each team is to win the regional &

national finals. 


The advantages for Cleveland Force include access to a very competitive and exclusive league for its

deserved players, U.S. Club affiliation- single player carding, a clear route to championship, college scouting

opportunities and preferred college showcase events, and all teams within the club have similar home and

away game schedules creating camaraderie between families/players and consistent coaching as compared

to the league we have participated in the past. 


The National Premier League was created to elevate and change the competitive youth soccer landscape by

extending developmental principles espoused by U.S. Soccer into more age groups and clubs, by linking

competition with player development and identification platforms and by providing meaningful weekly

competition culminating in the NPL Finals. NPLs are independent leagues, unified under one national

competition platform and based on a common technical framework designed to improve long-term player

development. 



The NPL Provides A Platform:


· Focused on long-term player development;


· For the country’s top soccer clubs, allowing consistent, meaningful high-level games appropriately

   scheduled with higher training-to-game ratios;


· That allows players to be scouted and evaluated by U.S. Soccer National Staff and Technical Advisors;


· That is designed and structured by the clubs, based on the needs of the clubs; and 


· That provides an avenue for qualification for the NPL Finals.



 
   
 

 

MIDWEST DEVELOPMENTAL LEAGUE

BOYS AGES U13 thru U19

The Midwest Developmental League, which includes 13-U, 14-U, 15-U, 16-U,

17-U and 19-U boys age groups, was founded in April 2011 as part of US Club

Soccer's National Premier Leagues (NPL).The 2018-19 Midwest Developmental

League includes an east and a west region, and is one of the qualifying leagues

under Elite National Premier League (ENPL). As part of the season schedules,

clubs will play within their conference and have the opportunity to play against

the opposite conference during a nationally recognized showcase.


 



   
   
   
 
   


 


GREAT LAKES ALLIANCE

BOYS AGES U13 thru U19

The Great Lake Alliance is a US Club Soccer sanctioned regional league

providing competition from a wide variety of cities throughout Ohio,

Western PA  and Western NY with convenient travel.

   
 
   
 

  

SUPER Y LEAGUE

BOYS AGES U12, U15, U17

GIRLS AGES U13, U14, U17

We are excited to announce that our Cleveland Force SC Boys Program will be

participating in Super Y League this Summer 2021!

The decision to enter the Super Y League was done with our commitment to

our Mission, Vision and Player Development Philosophy.

Super Y League provides our members with an experience that is

unparalleled in youth soccer. Super Y shares our passion for player development

and realizes that in order to be an elite club, we must play in an elite league. With

the new Regional Scouting Series, partnerships with some of the leaders in

youth soccer, experienced and professional staff, advanced player tracking, and

offering the chance to compete for a North American Championship, the Super Y

League is the best operated youth league in North America, allowing it to stand

above the competition.
