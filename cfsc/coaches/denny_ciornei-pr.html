Denny Ciornei Bio
  • 10 years of professional experience with Cleveland FORCE and Cleveland CRUNCH
  • 2-time NPSL champion
  • In the last 18 years, 100% of his students have continued their playing career at the collegiate level
  • Coached Charlie Horton, a goalkeeper at Leeds United England
  • Goalkeeping coach at Cleveland State University where he guided both men and women goalkeepers to Horizon league goalkeepers of the year in 2010.
  • Coached Brad Stuver at CSU who, for the first time in school history, was selected as the number one keeper drafted in the 2013 MLS super draft.
  • Coached Alec Ferrell who was also drafted as the number one keeper in the 2017 MLS super draft and was the ACC CHAMPION at Wake Forest University.
