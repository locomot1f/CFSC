Joe Raduka
CFSC Girls Staff Coach
Coaching Experience:
  • Founded Cleveland United Soccer Club in 1987
  • Assistant Coach Cleveland Crunch – Major Indoor Soccer League MISL (1989-1990)
  • Assistant Coach Cleveland Crunch – National Professional Soccer League NPSL (1998-1999)
  • Head Coach Ohio North Olympic Development (1998-2000)
  • Canton United Soccer Club (2008-present)
  • Ohio North Youth Coach of the Year (1998)
  • LEYPSL Youth Coach of the Year (1998)
  • Coach of ‘83 Cleveland United Boy’s Team Nike Cup Championship (1998)
  • Coach of ‘83 Cleveland United Boy’s Team ‘98 Youth World Cup Paris, France
  • Coach of ‘83 Cleveland United Boy’s team to the 1999 Region 2 Regional Championship – First boy’s team to achieve this title in Ohio North’s history
Playing Experience:
  • Red Star-Belgrade / Yugoslavian First Division (1968-1978)
  • Oakland Stompers / North American Soccer League NASL (1978-1979)
  • Edmonton Drillers / North American Soccer League NASL (1979-1981)
  • Namur (Belgium) on loan for 1979-1980 season
  • Golden Bay Earthquakes / Major Indoor Soccer League MISL (1982-1983)
  • Edmonton Eagles / Canadian Professional Soccer League CPSL (1983)
  • Cleveland Force / Major Indoor Soccer League MISL (1983-1987)
  • Captain of the Yugoslavian National Team (1971)
  • NSCAA National Youth Coach of the Year (1998)
